# Development Roadmap

## Phase 1 - Foundation
Establish the core platform, accounts, projects, documents, and the first workflow.

## Phase 2 - Engineering Intelligence
Introduce calculations, AI guidance, and knowledge capture.

## Phase 3 - Simulation Platform
Integrate simulation engines, result storage, and comparison workflows.

## Phase 4 - CAD and Prototyping
Add design asset management, CAD integration, and prototype planning.

## Phase 5 - Knowledge Network
Grow the knowledge graph, reusable components, and best-practice reuse.

## Phase 6 - Ecosystem
Open the platform to plugins, marketplace services, and community collaboration.
