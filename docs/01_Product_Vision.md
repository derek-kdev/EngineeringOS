# 01. Product Vision

## 1. Executive Summary
EngineeringOS is proposed as the world’s first comprehensive Engineering Operating System: a cloud-native platform that supports the full lifecycle of engineered work, from early concept through research, calculation, simulation, design, prototyping, testing, validation, documentation, and publication.

## 2. Mission
EngineeringOS exists to replace fragmented engineering tooling with a unified digital environment that preserves technical reasoning, evidence, and traceability throughout the product development pathway.

## 3. Strategic Opportunity
Modern engineering work is distributed across spreadsheets, CAD tools, simulation environments, document repositories, lab notebooks, and siloed research systems. This fragmentation creates version drift, loss of rationale, duplicated work, and delayed validation.

## 4. Product Vision Statement
EngineeringOS will become the authoritative collaborative environment in which an engineering idea becomes a validated prototype without leaving the platform ecosystem.

## 5. Core Value Proposition
- Unify research, engineering calculations, simulation, design, prototyping, and validation in one platform.
- Preserve technical intent and assumptions across the full lifecycle.
- Support collaboration between students, universities, researchers, startups, and industry.
- Create a reproducible engineering workspace for evidence-based innovation.

## 6. Target Users
- Students and educators
- Researchers and laboratories
- Engineers and technical teams
- Innovators and startups
- Industrial R&D organizations

## 7. Product Pillars
1. Engineering intelligence
2. Workflow traceability
3. Scientific reproducibility
4. Collaborative co-creation
5. Validation-centered design
6. Knowledge reuse

## 8. Success Criteria
- Faster time from concept to prototype
- Higher reuse of prior engineering assets
- Better traceability from requirement to test evidence
- Fewer design errors due to disconnected workflows
- Stronger adoption across academic and industrial environments

## 9. Scope of the First Platform Generation
The first generation will focus on the core engineering loop: project creation, research capture, calculations, simulation orchestration, design asset management, testing, and documentation.

## 10. Long-Term Strategic Direction
EngineeringOS will evolve into a living engineering ecosystem that combines human expertise, AI-assisted reasoning, simulation automation, digital twins, and validated workflow orchestration.
