# 14. Prototype Lab Architecture

## 1. Objective
The prototype lab architecture will provide a structured environment for converting validated designs into physical or experimental prototypes with controlled iteration and evidence capture.

## 2. Functional Domains
- Prototype versioning
- Assembly tracking
- Inventory and bill of materials
- Test logs and measurement records
- Failure analysis and lessons learned

## 3. Workflow Support
The lab system will coordinate build planning, fabrication status, testing, and result recording while maintaining connection to the originating design and validation process.

## 4. Governance and Traceability
Every prototype build must be traceable to a design revision, a component selection, and a validation objective.
