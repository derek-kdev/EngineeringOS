# 10. AI Architecture

## 1. AI Positioning
AI in EngineeringOS will serve as an engineering co-pilot that accelerates discovery, improves retrieval, and supports reasoning without replacing expert judgment.

## 2. AI Capabilities
- Research summarization and evidence extraction
- Document drafting and knowledge synthesis
- Calculation guidance and equation lookup
- Simulation setup support and parameter recommendations
- Design review assistance and anomaly explanation

## 3. Core AI Components
- Agent orchestration layer
- Knowledge retrieval system over project and organizational assets
- Prompt and policy management
- Model serving and evaluation framework
- Safety and guardrails infrastructure

## 4. Knowledge Integration
All AI outputs must be grounded in source evidence from the project, prior simulations, standards, formulas, and validated design records.

## 5. Governance and Safety
- Human review for significant recommendations
- Data access controls and policy enforcement
- Traceability from AI suggestions back to their evidence sources
